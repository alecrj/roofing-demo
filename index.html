Frequently
Asked Questions

Common questions about our roofing services and process

How long does a typical roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements take 2-4 days depending on size, weather, and material complexity. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Yes, we're experienced with insurance claims and work directly with adjusters to ensure proper coverage and streamlined approval processes.

What materials do you recommend for Colorado weather?

We recommend impact-resistant materials like architectural shingles, metal roofing, or tile that can withstand hail, wind, and temperature fluctuations.

How much does a new roof typically cost?

Costs vary by size, materials, and complexity. Most residential projects range from $12,000-$35,000. We provide detailed estimates during free consultations.

What's included in your warranty?

Our 25-year warranty covers materials, workmanship, and installation. It's transferable to new homeowners and includes storm damage protection.

Do you offer emergency roof repairs?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency response for storm damage, leaks, and urgent repairs throughout the Denver metro area.
